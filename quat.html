Quaternion Playground

One day I was playing with different multiplication tables for 1,i,j,k extension to numbers. And in the process made this JavaScript demo to see how different rules change behavior. I create a bunch (10000 default) initial quaternions that all are positioned around x=1 with random small deltas. Then I multiply them by themselves to see where they end up going.

Try setting K part to 0, shapes becomes a sphere. Also try changing signs in the multiplication table and other parameters on the left to play with results. You can rotate the dots and use scrool for scale.
